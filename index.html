About Me

Hi, I'm Palak — a passionate Shopify Developer with hands-on experience building and customizing high-performing eCommerce stores. I specialize in creating responsive, user-friendly Shopify websites tailored to each brand’s unique goals.


In addition to Shopify, I also have experience working with WordPress, Squarespace and other CMS platforms — helping clients build elegant, functional websites across platforms. Whether it's a custom Shopify theme, a WordPress business site, or a clean Squarespace layout, I ensure every project is crafted with performance, clarity, and conversion in mind.
